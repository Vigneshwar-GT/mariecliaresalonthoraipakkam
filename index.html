About Marie Claire Paris

Marie Claire is a leading international brand founded by Jean Prouvost, first published in France in 1937. Publishing in 28+ countries across 5 continents, reaching 17 million readers monthly with 26 million+ social media followers.

Based on its worldwide popularity, Marie Claire holds an extended licensing program with 50+ global partners covering fashion, cosmetics, perfumes, and beauty services.

B2C Network LLP — India Licensee

B2C Network LLP is the exclusive India Licensee for Marie Claire Paris. Our founders have 15+ years of experience operating 200+ outlets across Salons, Spas, and Vocational Academies.
